FAQs

How do I register?

To register, simply visit our website and click on the 'Sign Up' button. Fill out the required information, including your nutritional goals and preferences. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

What is the premium plan?

The premium plan offers advanced features such as personalized meal plans, exclusive recipes, and detailed nutritional insights. Subscribers also gain access to priority customer support. It's designed for users seeking a more tailored experience.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We prioritize your privacy and security. All user data is encrypted and stored securely, ensuring that your information remains confidential.
